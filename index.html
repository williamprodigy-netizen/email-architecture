THE BIG MOVE

Your current Skool 7-day = 7 emails. Ericson would say not enough. The Ericson move: every new opt-in (webinar reg, Skool join, VSL view) enters a 30-email tailored welcome over 3-4 weeks.

Phase 1 (E1-4, 2/day): case study + reply-farm Q + PROOF stack + 12-Q FAQ. Phase 2 (E5-15, daily): reuse your 10 best-ever broadcasts as the welcome series. Phase 3 (E16-30, daily): deep stories + how-to + soft asks. THEN graduate to daily broadcasts.

Effect: every opt-in gets a personalized 4-week pitch sequence instead of generic nurture. Stop nurturing — start tailored selling.

RULE: a contact is always in exactly ONE flow tag. State changes swap flows. BOTTLENECK: lead→book at 6.5% — Flow A & B recoup catch the 93.5% who drop, push them to evergreen instead of dying. KEYSTONE: RPSM (revenue per subscriber per month). Open rate is dead (Apple MPP). Track click + reply + revenue, not opens.
